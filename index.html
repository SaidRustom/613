§ 02 — The House

A capital institution.

Bank St. · Ottawa


613 was built on a simple idea: a haircut should leave a man sharper than he arrived. It's a small ritual, and it should be done right.

We sit where barbershop tradition meets the pace of Ottawa — cutting hair for civil servants, tradesmen, students, founders, and everyone between.

The chairs are vintage. The blades are sharp. The conversation is yours to start or skip. We take our time, watch the mirror with you, and we don't let you leave until the line is right.


The cut is the easy part. The standard is everything else.

Established

Ottawa, on the 613.

Skin fades, scissor work, hot shaves — in that order.
